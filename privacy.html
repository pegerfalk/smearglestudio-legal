Privacy Policy — SmeargleStudio Publisher

Last updated: 21 July 2026

SmeargleStudio Publisher ("the App") is an internal tool operated by SmeargleStudio (Sweden). It publishes SmeargleStudio's own videos to SmeargleStudio's own TikTok account and reads statistics for those videos. This policy explains what data the App handles.

1. Whose data the App processes

Only SmeargleStudio's own. The App connects exclusively to TikTok accounts owned and operated by SmeargleStudio. It does not collect, process, or store any data about other TikTok users, viewers, or any member of the public.

2. What data is stored

  • Authentication tokens issued by TikTok for the connected account(s), used solely to publish videos and read statistics.
  • Video metadata and statistics for SmeargleStudio's own posts (video id, caption, view/like/comment/share counts), used to decide which designs to feature next.

No names, contact details, device identifiers, or behavioural data of any third party are collected.

3. Where and how long

Data is stored on a private server operated by SmeargleStudio within the EU (Stockholm, Sweden), accessible only to the owner. Tokens are kept for as long as the account connection is active; statistics are kept as long as they are useful for planning content. Either can be deleted at any time on request.

4. Sharing

Nothing is sold or shared with any third party. Data goes to exactly one external service: TikTok's official API, as required to publish videos and read statistics.

5. Your rights

As the App processes no third-party personal data, no third party is affected. For any question or request regarding data handled by the App, contact the operator below; requests are honoured under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
