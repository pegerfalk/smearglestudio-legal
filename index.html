SmeargleStudio Publisher

SmeargleStudio Publisher is the internal publishing tool of SmeargleStudio, an independent art studio in Sweden creating printable "extended art" binder pages for trading-card collectors.

The tool publishes SmeargleStudio's own promotional videos to SmeargleStudio's own TikTok account and reads viewing statistics for those videos, helping the studio decide which artwork to feature next. It is operated solely by the studio's owner and is not offered to the public.
